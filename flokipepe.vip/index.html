How to Buy $XNXX

Memecoin has had a meteoric rise in popularity in the cryptocurrency market in recent years, attracting significant investment and having a startling total market capitalization of billions of dollars.

Step 1 - Create MetaMask wallet

Create a MetaMask Wallet using either a desktop computer or an iOS/Android mobile device. That will allow you to buy, sell, send, and receive FlokiPepe

Step 2 - Send BNB to your wallet

You can transfer it to your MetaMask Wallet from exchanges like Coinbase, Binance, etc. Make sure to use the BEP-20 network when transferring BNB.

Step 3 - Connect your wallet to Pancakeswap

Access your wallet to Pancakeswap by clicking ‘Connect Wallet’ and selecting MetaMask.

Step 4 - Swap BNB for FlokiPepe

You can start swapping as soon as you have BNB available! Press ‘Select a currency’ and enter the FlokiPepe contract.
